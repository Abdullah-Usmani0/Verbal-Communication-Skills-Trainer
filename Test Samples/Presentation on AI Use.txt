Presentation Script: How AI is Helping People

[Slide 1: Title Slide]
Hello everyone,
Today, I'll be talking about how Artificial Intelligence is helping people and transforming our daily lives.

[Slide 2: What is AI?]
AI, or Artificial Intelligence, refers to machines or software that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence — such as understanding language, recognizing patterns, and making decisions.

[Slide 3: How AI is Helping People]
AI is making a positive impact across various fields:

In Healthcare, AI helps doctors diagnose diseases faster and more accurately.
In Education, it provides personalized learning experiences for students.
In Accessibility, AI tools like voice assistants and text-to-speech help people with disabilities communicate more easily.
In Customer Service, chatbots are available 24/7 to answer questions and solve problems.
[Slide 4: Real-Life Example]
A great example is Google's AI-powered flood forecasting system, which warns communities about floods before they happen — saving lives and resources.

[Slide 5: The Future of AI]
AI is still evolving, but its potential to improve lives is limitless. From fighting climate change to helping people with mental health issues, the future of AI looks promising.

[Slide 6: Conclusion]
AI is not just about machines — it's about helping people live better, healthier, and more connected lives. As technology advances, it's important to ensure AI remains ethical, fair, and accessible to everyone.

Thank you!
